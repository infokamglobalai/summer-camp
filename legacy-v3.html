FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers! 🙋

🤔Does my child need coding experience?

Not at all! Everything starts from scratch. Our camp is designed for complete beginners in Grades 6–12 — all you need is curiosity and a laptop!

⏰Do students attend both daily sessions?

No! Morning (10AM) and evening (7PM) cover the same content. Students pick one — or mix across days. It's totally flexible for busy family schedules.

💻What equipment is needed?

Just a laptop or desktop with internet. All tools are free and browser-based — no downloads! We walk through setup together on Day 1.

💳Is ₹99 really the total cost?

100% yes! ₹99 covers all 10 days, all 20 sessions, every activity, materials, mentorship, and the certificate ceremony. Zero hidden costs.

🏅Will my child get a certificate?

Absolutely! Every student who completes all 10 days receives a digital Certificate of Completion at the AI Innovation Showcase — perfect for school portfolios!

👫Can my child join with a friend?

Yes! We love friend groups — learning together makes it more fun. Enroll together and request the same project team in your registration form.
